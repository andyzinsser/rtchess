Bullet Chess is the first demo of Arbiter in action

Bullet Chess uses Arbiter to manage the wagered bitcoin during the game. You and your opponent will buy into a wallet managed my Arbiter (via Coinbase). Once there is a winner, the funds will get released to that winner (minus a 1% fee on the transaction).

You'll need a Coinbase account

Arbiter is built ontop of Coinbase for transferring the Bitcoin between players. So, you will need to create an account at Coinbase.com.
